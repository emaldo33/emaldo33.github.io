Godizlla Monsters

Godzilla has many monsters that have appeared in the movies, some are allies and some are enemies. Here is a list of some of the most popular monsters that have appeared in the Godzilla franchise.





*An a link to the image of each monster is provided next to its description.

  • King Ghidorah - A three-headed dragon and one of Godzilla's most powerful enemies.
    First appeared in "Ghidorah, the Three-Headed Monster" (1964). King Ghidorah

  • Mothra - A giant moth-like creature and one of Godzilla's most famous allies.
    First appeared in "Mothra" (1961). Mothra

  • Rodan - A giant pterosaur-like creature and occasional ally of Godzilla.
    First appeared in "Rodan" (1956). Rodan

  • Mechagodzilla - A robotic version of Godzilla created by humans to combat the real Godzilla.
    First appeared in "Godzilla vs. Mechagodzilla" (1974). Mechagodzilla

  • Gigan - A cyborg monster with a buzzsaw in its chest and hooks for hands.
    First appeared in "Godzilla vs. Gigan" (1972). Gigan

  • Anguirus - A spiked dinosaur-like creature and one of Godzilla's earliest foes.
    First appeared in "Godzilla Raids Again" (1955). Anguirus

  • Biollante - A giant plant-like monster created from Godzilla's cells.
    First appeared in "Godzilla vs. Biollante" (1989). Biollante

  • Space Godzilla - A space-faring version of Godzilla with crystalline structures on its body.
    First appeared in "Godzilla vs. Space Godzilla" (1994). Space Godzilla

  • Destoroyah - A crustacean-like monster born from the Oxygen Destroyer that killed the original Godzilla.
    First appeared in "Godzilla vs. Destoroyah" (1995). Destoroyah
